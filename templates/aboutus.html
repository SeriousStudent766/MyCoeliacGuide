What Is Coeliac Disease?

Coeliac disease is a serious autoimmune condition where the body reacts to gluten, damaging the small intestine. It affects around 1 in 100 people in the UK. Symptoms vary but can include bloating, fatigue, weight loss, and digestive discomfort. The only treatment is a strict gluten-free diet.

My Coeliac Guide helps make this journey easier by offering tools that track gluten exposure, monitor symptoms, and support lifestyle changes.
