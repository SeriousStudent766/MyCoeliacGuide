Our Mission

At My Coeliac Guide, our mission is to empower individuals living with coeliac disease by giving them the tools, confidence, and community they need to take control of their health. We understand the daily challenges of maintaining a strict gluten-free lifestyle, and we’re here to make that journey easier and more manageable.

Whether you're newly diagnosed or have been living with coeliac for years, our platform is designed to support you every step of the way. From logging your meals and tracking symptoms to accessing gluten-free recipes and engaging with a supportive community, we’ve built this space to help you live well — your way.

We’re not just a tracking tool — we’re a companion on your journey. Our goal is to help reduce confusion, avoid gluten exposure, and promote better long-term health through simple, user-focused features. With My Coeliac Guide, you’re never navigating this path alone.

Because managing coeliac disease shouldn’t be complicated — it should be empowering.
